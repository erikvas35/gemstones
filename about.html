About Our Team

Dedicated Researchers Advancing PCOS Detection

Our Team

Team AI-PCOS is a student research group on the University of Maryland College Park! We are in the Gemstone honors program on campus and we are looking into causes of PCOS in women and providing at home help for women from low income communities. We want to develop an artificial intelligence that works with medical records to help detect PCOS!
